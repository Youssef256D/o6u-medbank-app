# O6U MedBank — Official Launch Announcement

**Date:** March 13, 2026

---

We are excited to announce the official launch of **O6U MedBank**, the dedicated MCQ practice platform built for students of the **October 6 University Faculty of Medicine**.

Whether you're preparing for a midterm, a final, or simply reinforcing your knowledge, O6U MedBank gives you the tools to study smarter and track your progress with confidence.

---

## What is O6U MedBank?

O6U MedBank is a browser-based practice platform that lets you build focused, exam-style MCQ tests from your course material — no installs, no downloads, just open and study.

---

## Key Features

### Build Your Own Tests
Create custom practice tests by selecting your course and topic. Configure question count, choose between **Timed** (exam simulation) or **Tutor** (instant feedback) mode, and decide whether to pull from all questions, unused questions, your incorrect queue, or flagged items.

### Realistic Exam Interface
Answer questions under exam conditions with tools you actually need:
- Flag questions to revisit later
- Strike through options you've eliminated
- Add personal notes per question
- Jump between questions freely
- Pause and resume the timer

### Detailed Review & Explanations
After every test, get a full score breakdown with per-question explanations, so you understand not just what the right answer is but why.

### Performance Tracking
The platform tracks your history across tests, identifies weak areas, and maintains an **incorrect queue** so you can keep revisiting questions until you get them right.

### Flashcards
Reinforce key concepts with the built-in flashcard system, separate from the test flow for quick revision sessions.

### Notifications
Stay informed with an in-app notification system that keeps you updated on new content and platform activity in real time.

### Multi-Theme Support
Study comfortably with three visual themes: **Light**, **Dark**, and **Comfort** — switch anytime from your profile.

### Works Offline
O6U MedBank is a Progressive Web App (PWA). You can install it on your device and continue studying even without an internet connection.
