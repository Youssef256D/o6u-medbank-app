# Support Policy

Effective date: June 30, 2026

MedBank support helps with account access, course enrollment, question-bank
issues, technical problems, data requests, and administrator workflows.

## What support can help with

Support can help with:

- login, signup, password reset, and account recovery issues;
- approval, deactivation, or course-access questions;
- missing or incorrect course assignments;
- question-bank issues such as broken choices, missing explanations, duplicate
  questions, image problems, or topic mismatch;
- in-progress session or previous-test display problems;
- course-platform access, lesson, video, or material issues;
- notification, profile, analytics, or browser-cache problems;
- admin workflows such as user access, imports, course setup, announcements,
  and audit-log questions;
- privacy, correction, export, and deletion requests.

## What support cannot do

Support cannot provide emergency help, medical advice, diagnosis, treatment
guidance, official exam rulings, or guaranteed academic outcomes.

Support cannot restore previous-test history after the 20-day retention window
has passed. Support also cannot guarantee recovery of data that was never
successfully synced to hosted Supabase.

Support cannot grant course access unless the request is approved through the
appropriate administrator or course owner.

## How to contact support

Use the MedBank Contact or Support page. If you are signed in, include the email
address connected to your account. If you cannot sign in, use the email address
you used to register.

For faster help, include:

- your account email;
- the affected course and topic, if relevant;
- the page or feature where the issue happened;
- what you expected to happen;
- what actually happened;
- screenshots or exact error messages when safe to share;
- the approximate date and time of the issue;
- whether you were on desktop, mobile browser, or installed/PWA mode.

Do not send passwords, service-role keys, agent tokens, provider API keys,
payment information, or patient-identifiable health information.

## Priorities

Support requests are generally handled in this order:

1. account lockout, security, privacy, and deletion requests;
2. active course-access problems that block studying;
3. question-bank or course-material errors affecting many students;
4. individual session, analytics, or browser issues;
5. product feedback and general questions.

## Security reports

If you believe you found a security issue, report it through support with a
clear description and steps to reproduce. Do not exploit the issue, access
another user's data, download data that is not yours, or publicly disclose the
issue before it has been reviewed.

## Data requests

For account deletion and personal-data deletion, follow the Data Deletion
Instructions. Support may need to verify account ownership before completing a
privacy or deletion request.

## Browser troubleshooting

Because MedBank is a static browser app with offline/cache support, some issues
can be caused by stale local browser data. Support may ask you to refresh the
page, sign out and back in, clear site data, try a private window, or test a
different browser. Hosted Supabase remains the source of truth for account,
course, question, enrollment, and synced progress data.
